Exercises and Diet for Unhealthy Weight

Exercises

Regular exercise is important for maintaining a healthy weight. Aim to get at least 150 minutes of moderate intensity or 75 minutes of vigorous intensity exercise each week, or a combination of both. Examples of exercises include:

  • Walking
  • Running
  • Cycling
  • Swimming
  • Strength training

Diet

Eating a healthy and balanced diet is also important for maintaining a healthy weight. Aim to eat a variety of foods from all food groups, including:

  • Fruits and vegetables
  • Whole grains
  • Proteins (e.g., lean meat, poultry, fish, legumes)
  • Low-fat dairy products
  • Healthy fats (e.g., nuts, seeds, avocado)

Limit your intake of added sugars, saturated and trans fats, and sodium.
